## Description (4000 chars max)

Battle Bee Game Tracker is the ultimate life tracker for Commander format card games — built for the table, designed for four players.

**Track everything that matters**
Keep life totals, poison counters, and commander damage for every player at once — all on one screen. No more paper, no more confusion.

**Built for the table**
Each player gets their own quadrant, rotated to face them. Tap the left or right half of your panel to adjust life. Hold for a quick +/- menu. Pull down your panel to track poison and commander damage — all without interrupting the game.

**2, 4, or 6 players**
Switch between 2-player, 4-player, and 6-player modes instantly. Each layout rotates player panels so everyone can read their own info from their seat.

**Customise your table**
- Set any starting life total — 20, 30, 40, 60, or a custom number
- Choose from 6 app colour themes: Arcane, Ocean, Inferno, Nature, Gold, Void
- Pick a GIF background for each player from GIPHY
- Give each player a custom emoji and name

**Commander-ready**
- Track commander damage received from each opponent
- 21 commander damage auto-eliminates a player
- Poison counter tracking (10 = eliminated)
- Explosion animation and sound when a player is eliminated

**Game history & stats**
Log completed games with deck names, commanders, and a winner. View your win rates, favourite commanders, and leaderboard over time.

**Who goes first?**
Use the built-in finger chooser — every player puts a finger on the screen and one is randomly chosen. Fair, fast, fun.

**No account required. No ads. No internet needed.** All data stays on your device.
